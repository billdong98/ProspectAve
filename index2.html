Have you ever wanted to go out to The Street but weren't sure which eating clubs were open? Have you ever wanted a pass but asked your friends too late? Well ProspectAve is the app for you! ProspectAve is Princeton's only centralized platform that allows students to quickly find which eating clubs are open and what they need to get in. No longer do you have to ask friends which clubs are open. To plan a prospective night out, head on over to ProspectAve today!
