ABOUT

ProspectAve was designed and developed for COS 333: Advanced Programming Techniques during the Spring '18 semester.

ProspectAve is a centralized platform for students to efficiently find information on eating clubs. Use the icons above to view which clubs are open and what is required for entry. Click on the colored icons to see more information on the event. Use the buttons or calendar to change the date and update the icons!
