ABOUT

ProspectAve was designed and developed for COS 333: Advanced Programming Techniques during the Spring '18 semester.

ProspectAve is a centralized platform for students to efficiently find information on eating clubs. Use the map to view which clubs are open and what is required for entry. Mouse-over colored clubs to see more information on the event. Use the buttons or calendar to change the date and update the map!
