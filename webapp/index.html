ABOUT

ProspectAve was designed and developed for COS 333: Advanced Programming Techniques during the Spring '18 semester.

ProspectAve was designed as a centralized platform for students to efficiently find information on eating clubs. Navigate to a certain date and use the map to view which clubs are open and what is needed for entry.
